Menu

Pizza

Pizza - a dish of Italian cuisine, now widely distributed around the world. It is a flat pancake made of yeast dough (focaccia), topped with tomato sauce, sprinkled with grated cheese (usually mozzarella) and herbs, baked in a very hot oven.

Pancakes

Pancake - a simple flour dish fried in a pan. Served pancakes can be sprinkled with sugar, powdered sugar or poured with sauce, although they are most often served with a filling, such as jam, sliced fruit, jam, cottage cheese, nutella or meat.

Pasta

Pasta as spaghetti. What is spaghetti? Spaghetti is a hot dish based on spaghetti noodles, served with tomato, cheese or other sauce. Spaghetti pasta is long (as long as 80 cm) strands made from durum wheat, a hard variety of wheat.
